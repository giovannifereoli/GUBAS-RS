Mutual gravitational potential and its partial derivatives (Hou 2016).

All functions are generic over Scalar so that Dual types can flow through for STM / parameter-sensitivity computation. Units: km, kg, s.

FunctionPurpose
potentialTotal mutual potential energy
du_x∂U/∂r — used for translational EOM
du_c∂U/∂C — one rotation-matrix column, used for torques
